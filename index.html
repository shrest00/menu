Brunch at Emily's

A 5-Course Culinary Journey

1. Avocado Toast

Toasted sourdough topped with smashed avocado, cherry tomatoes, chili flakes, and a sprinkle of feta.

2. Classic Mimosa

Chilled champagne blended with fresh-squeezed orange juice. Garnish with a twist of orange peel.

3. Eggs Benedict

Poached eggs over English muffins with Canadian bacon, topped with creamy hollandaise sauce.

4. Fresh Fruit Tart

Sweet pastry crust filled with vanilla custard, topped with sliced kiwi, berries, and glazed with apricot jam.

5. Espresso Finale

Rich espresso served in a demitasse with a square of dark chocolate on the side. Perfect ending!
